Turkey Gravy

Description

It's the gravy made from turkeys. The turkeys work very hard making this gravy.

Ingredients

  • turkey drippings
  • water
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Let the turkey drip all over
  2. Take the dripping and rub it with butter
  3. Add flour and look for the wet spot
  4. Add the salt and pepper
