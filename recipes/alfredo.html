Alfredo

Description

This delicious sauce only requires a few ingredients and loved by many.

Ingredients

  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan
  2. Stir in the flour
  3. Cook a few minutes until it has a paste-like consistency
  4. Stir in the cream and cook while stiring until the sauce thickens to desired thickness
